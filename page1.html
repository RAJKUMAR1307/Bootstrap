About Sethu Institute of Technology, Kariapatti

Sethu Institute of Technology (SIT), Kariapatti is an autonomous institute and was established in the year 1995 by the Sethu Educational Trust. The institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). SIT courses include undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech) programmes in various areas. Sethu Institute of Technology is affiliated to Anna University.
